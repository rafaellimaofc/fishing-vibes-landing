Fishing is like life: not always a catch, but always a story.

Fishing is about much more than just catching a fish. It’s about waking up early, breathing in the scent of nature, listening to the sound of the water, and living moments that stay in your memory. Every trip is a new story, a lesson, and a chance to renew your soul.

TOP 3 FISH

Peacock Bass The Peacock Bass is famous for its aggressive strikes and powerful fights. Found mainly in the Amazon, it loves topwater lures and guarantees exciting action

Golden Dorado The Golden Dorado is known for its speed and acrobatic jumps. Strong and fast, it challenges any angler and is a true trophy in Brazilian rivers.

Trahira The Trahira surprises with sudden attacks near the banks. Small but strong, it’s great fun on light tackle, especially with surface lures.

Fishing lure

My favorite artificial baits.

Xuxinha

Artificial lure Jig Xuxinha: It is known for its compact and attractive design, which imitates small fish or shrimp, making it irresistible to fish.

Brava

Artificial lure Brava: The Brava is known for its vibrant and realistic swimming action, which attracts fish even in murky waters or strong currents.

Tontinha

Artificial lure Tontinha: The Tontinha stands out for its erratic and fast movement in the water, which simulates the behavior of a frightened prey, triggering the fish’s attack instinct.
